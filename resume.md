Robotics Engineer specializing in autonomy, perception, and embedded AI deployment on NVIDIA Jetson platforms. I design and ship end-to-end robotic systems spanning simulation validation, ROS 2 autonomy integration, real-time vision pipelines, and TensorRT/CUDA optimization under embedded compute constraints. My focus is on measurable performance gains, deployment reliability, and scalable multi-robot software architectures.
